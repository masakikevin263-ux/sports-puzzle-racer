# sports-puzzle-racer
A fast-paced browser racing game where players solve math and sports puzzles to boost their car and beat opponents to the finish line.
## 🎮 Gameplay

- Solve math and sports questions while racing
- Correct answers give speed boosts 🚀
- Wrong answers slow you down 💨 and reduce lives ❤️
- Beat the opponent before they reach the finish line 🏁

---

## 🧠 Features

- Math + sports trivia challenges
- Combo system for bonus points
- Dynamic difficulty progression
- Opponent AI racing system
- Timer-based puzzle pressure
- Mobile + desktop support

---

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open the game in your browser
2. Start the race
3. Answer puzzles quickly and correctly
4. Build combos for higher scores
5. Reach the finish line before your opponent
